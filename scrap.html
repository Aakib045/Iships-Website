Commodities

What we buy.

Cu

Copper

Bare bright, #1 & #2 copper wire, tubing, busbars, beryllium, and insulated cable — all grades accepted.

Al

Aluminium

Extrusions, cast, sheet, radiators, and mixed clips. Painted or unpainted — we grade on-site.

Br

Brass

Yellow brass, red brass, cartridge cases, plumbing fittings, and mixed brass — all commercial grades.

Fe

HMS Scrap

Heavy Melting Steel #1 and #2, bundled or loose. Large volumes preferred — we arrange pickup.

SS

Stainless Steel

Grades 304, 316, 430, and other austenitic stainless. Cut pieces, turnings, and solids all considered.

Mx

Mixed Metals

Insulated wire, electric motors, transformers, e-scrap, and mixed non-ferrous lots. Priced per assay.
