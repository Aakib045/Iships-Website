Our Story

One house.
Three disciplines.
Ten years.

Iships General Trading was founded to fill a gap in the Gulf market: a single, licensed trading house that could handle metals, machinery parts, and global cargo under one roof — without the layers of middlemen that slow everything down.

Based in Dubai, we've spent a decade building supplier and buyer relationships across more than forty countries. Our three divisions — Scrap Trading, Spare Parts, and Import/Export — operate independently but share the same standard: honest pricing, fast execution, and full documentation from start to finish.


Every trade we do is backed by our UAE general trading licence. We're not brokers. We take ownership of every deal from first call to final settlement.
